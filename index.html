FAQ

  • What are the specs?

    The insoles have a base height of 3cm (1.18 in) with two optional 1.5cm (0.59 in) layers for a max elevation of 6cm (2.36 in). They weigh 4.7 oz.

  • Are these insoles compatible with all types of shoes?

    The insoles may fit into low-top shoes using just the base. However, for an optimal comfort fit, high-tops are recommended. To comfortably accommodate the full 6cm elevation, a larger shoe size is highly suggested.

  • Will people notice I'm wearing height insoles?

    The insoles are discreetly designed to go unnoticed, and with the right shoes, they're virtually undetectable.

  • How much height will I actually gain?

    While a 2.36" insole is designed to add as much to your height, the actual gain may be less. This is due to factors like body weight - which compresses the insole - and the precise positioning of the insole within the shoe.

  • Can I comfortably use these insoles for an entire day?

    Initially, it is common to find extended wear of insoles at the full 6cm elevation to be challenging. To ensure comfort, begin with the base height of 3cm and gradually add the 1.5cm layers as you acclimate to the additional elevation. Allow your feet and posture time to adjust by increasing height in increments and limiting wear time at each new level. Practice a natural heel-to-toe walking pattern, maintain an upright posture, and consider shorter steps to ease the transition. By gradually increasing the height and ensuring a proper fit, these insoles can help you find a comfortable balance for extended use throughout the day.
